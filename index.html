Did You Know?

Yoda's species has never been named in Star Wars lore.
The sound of a TIE fighter was created using an elephant's trumpet mixed with a car driving on wet pavement.
Chewbacca's voice was a mix of bear, walrus, lion, and badger sounds.
The Star Wars universe spans over 25,000 years of fictional history.
Luke Skywalker was originally named Luke Starkiller in early drafts of the script.
The Millennium Falcon design was inspired by a hamburger with an olive on the side.
