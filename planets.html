Endor

Endor is a forested moon orbiting the gas giant of the same name in the Outer Rim. It is home to the Ewok species, whose bravery helped the Rebel Alliance defeat the Empire in the Battle of Endor, leading to the fall of the second Death Star.

Coruscant

Coruscant is a bustling metropolis located at the center of the galaxy. It is the political heart of the Republic, home to the Jedi Temple, and the seat of the Galactic Senate. Its towering skyscrapers cover the entire surface, a city that never sleeps.

Hoth

Hoth is an icy, remote planet located in the Outer Rim, known for its freezing conditions and vast snowfields. The Rebel Alliance established a base there during the Galactic Civil War, though it was eventually discovered by the Empire.

Mustafar

Mustafar is a volcanic planet located in the Outer Rim, notorious for its rivers of lava and fiery terrain. It became the site of the iconic duel between Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi, where Anakin’s transformation into Darth Vader was sealed.

Jakku

Jakku is a remote desert planet located in the Western Reaches of the galaxy. It was the home of Rey, the scavenger who would eventually play a key role in the battle against the First Order.

Kamino

Kamino is a water-covered planet located in the Outer Rim, known for its towering storm clouds and oceanic environment. It is where the clone army was created, making it an essential location for the rise of the Galactic Republic.

Tatooine

Tatooine is a harsh desert planet located in the Outer Rim Territories, infamous for its twin suns and extreme climate. It is the home planet of Anakin Skywalker and Luke Skywalker, playing a critical role in their destinies.

Alderaan

Alderaan was a peaceful and affluent planet located in the Core Worlds, known for its beautiful landscapes and strong cultural and educational institutions. Unfortunately, it was destroyed by the Empire's Death Star in Episode IV, marking a pivotal moment in the Star Wars saga.

Naboo

Naboo is a lush, serene planet located in the Mid Rim, known for its striking natural beauty, vast plains, and expansive lakes. The planet was home to Queen Padmé Amidala and the famous Gungan species, including Jar Jar Binks.
