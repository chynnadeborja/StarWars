Lightsabers: The Elegant Weapon

Lightsabers are the weapon of choice for Jedi, Sith, and others sensitive to the Force. Each blade is unique, reflecting the personality and allegiance of its wielder.

Blue: Courage and Protection

Green: Harmony and Wisdom

Red: Power and Control

Fun Facts

  • The kyber crystals powering lightsabers are naturally colorless and take on a color based on their connection to the user.
  • Lightsaber combat has seven traditional forms, each suited for different styles of battle.
  • The rare black-bladed Darksaber is a unique weapon created by the first Mandalorian Jedi.
