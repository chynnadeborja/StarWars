Lightsaber Lore

Blue Lightsaber

The blue lightsaber symbolizes courage and protection. It is the traditional weapon of Jedi Guardians who focus on physical combat and defense.

Notable User: Obi Wan Kenobi

Green Lightsaber

The green lightsaber represents harmony and wisdom. It is commonly used by Jedi Consulars who focus on diplomacy and the Force.
