Lightsaber Lore

Blue Lightsaber

The blue lightsaber symbolizes courage and protection. It is the traditional weapon of Jedi Guardians who focus on physical combat and defense.

Notable User: Obi Wan Kenobi

Green Lightsaber

The green lightsaber represents harmony and wisdom. It is commonly used by Jedi Consulars who focus on diplomacy and the Force.

Notable User: Master Yoda

Red Lightsaber

Red lightsabers, symbolize their allegiance to the dark side. Restricted from using natural Kyber crystals, the Sith created synthetic ones or tainted Jedi crystals through dark rituals, causing them to "bleed" and emit a crimson hue. This iconic blade color has been the hallmark of Sith throughout Star Wars history, enhancing their fearsome presence.

Notable User: Sith Lords like Darth Vader, Darth Maul, and the Grand Inquisitor,

OrangeLightsaber

Orange lightsabers, rare in Star Wars lore, have been associated with "gray" Force users who reject alignment with either the Jedi or Sith in Legends content. Their exact significance remains unclear but hints at a unique moral or Force alignment.

Notable User: Yaddle, Plo Koon, Baylan Skoll, and Shin Hati

Yellow Lightsaber

Yellow lightsabers, wielded by Jedi Sentinels, represent a balance between the combat focus of Guardians and the wisdom of Consulars. Sentinels often used practical skills beyond the Force to serve and protect others. They frequently guarded Jedi Temples with yellow double-bladed lightsabers.

Notable User: Rey Skywalker

Purple Lightsaber

Purple lightsabers, requested by Samuel L. Jackson to make his character stand out. In Star Wars lore, purple blades symbolize a balance between the light and dark sides of the Force, reflecting Windu's ability to resist dark side temptations while mastering the light. This rare blade highlights his unique strength and understanding of the Force.

Notable User: Master Windu

White Lightsaber

White lightsabers, first used by the Imperial Knights in Star Wars Legends, symbolize a rejection of traditional light and dark side beliefs. Ahsoka Tano later wielded white-bladed lightsabers after leaving the Jedi Order, representing her independent path in serving the Force. She created them by purifying red Kyber crystals taken from defeated Imperial Inquisitors, showcasing her strength and neutrality during and after the Clone Wars.

Notable User: Ahsoka Tano

Darksaber

The Darksaber, a one-of-a-kind black-bladed lightsaber, was created by Tarre Vizsla, the only Mandalorian Jedi. Featuring a shorter, flat blade and a hilt made of beskar, it requires a mental connection with its wielder to be used effectively.

Notable User: Bo-Katan, Darth Maul, Moff Gideon, and Din Djarin
